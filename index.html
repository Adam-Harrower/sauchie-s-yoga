The Benefits Of Yoga

Heart Benefits

Yoga can help lower blood pressure, cholesterol and blood suger, all of which are good for your heart and blood vessels.

Fitness Benefits

Practicing yoga a couple of times a week increases muscle strength and flexibility, boosts endurance, and tunes up your heart and lungs.

Eating Benefits

Being more mindful of how your body feels carries over to mealtimes as you sevor each bite or sipand notice how food smells, tastes, and feels in your mouth.

Weight Benefits

Mindfulness developed through yoga can make you more sebsitive to cues of hunger and fullness, which help you develop a more positive relationship with food.
